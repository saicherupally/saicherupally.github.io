Domain Expertise

Digital Design

Front-end RTL implementation of complex digital hardware for ASIC applications including machine learning, hardware security, hardware accelerators, and special purpose processors.

Functional Verification

Different types of functional verification based on scoreboards, checkers, golden models, end-to-end register state comparison, simple assertions, and constrained randomization.

Analog/Mixed-signal Design

Optimization of deep neural networks for deployment on mixed-signal in-memory computing hardware, cross-validation using implementation of digital equivalents, and high level modelling in python/pytorch.

Hardware-aware Deep Neural Networks

Making deep neural networks robust to noise observed in in-memory computing hardware, and leveraging this noise to defend deep neural networks against adversarial input and weight attacks.
